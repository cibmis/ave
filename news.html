New resource for ID theft victims
May 21, 2015

The Federal Trade Commission has announced the launch of a new website, IdentityTheft.gov, a resource that makes it easier for victims to report and recover from identity theft. The new website provides an interactive checklist that walks people through the recovery process and helps them understand which recovery steps should be taken upon learning their identity has been stolen. It also provides sample letters, specialized tips for specific forms of identity theft, and advice for people who have been notified that their personal information was exposed in a data breach. A Spanish version of the site is also available.

CIBM Bank Opens Doors of New Branch in Wheaton, Illinois
May 18, 2015

CHAMPAIGN, IL--(Marketwired - May 18, 2015) - Today, CIBM Bank proudly opens the doors of its newest branch location at 330 S. Naperville Road, Suite 100, Wheaton, Illinois. The full-service branch operates under the name of "Avenue Bank, a branch of CIBM Bank."

"We are excited to build upon the strong relationships our Avenue Mortgage division has developed in the Chicagoland market and look forward to serving the Wheaton community with our full array of personal banking, corporate banking and treasury management services," said Mr. John Hickey, CIBM Bank's President and CEO. "We're pleased that Bill Mucker has accepted the role of Branch Manager at the Wheaton office. Bill has more than 25 years banking experience in the Chicagoland market and has most recently worked in the Wheaton, Naperville and Downers Grove areas. Bill is a solid addition to CIBM Bank as the leader of our Wheaton team."

CIBM Bank is a wholly owned subsidiary of CIB Marine Bancshares, Inc. that operates as "Marine Bank" in its Indiana and Wisconsin markets, "Central Illinois Bank" in its central Illinois market, and "Avenue Bank" in its Chicagoland market. The Avenue Mortgage division, located in Naperville, Illinois serves all CIBM Bank markets. To view employment openings visit www.cibmcareers.com. Equal housing lender. Member FDIC.
